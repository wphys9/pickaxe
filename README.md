## What Is Pickaxe, Actually?

Pickaxe is a no-code platform to build, launch, and sell AI products. The basic unit of the platform is called a "Pickaxe" ‚Äî which is just their name for an AI tool you create. A Pickaxe is an AI tool, either a chatbot or a form, that is powered with a prompt. In addition to the prompt, you can power your Pickaxe with documents, websites, videos, special instructions, API endpoints, and more.

Think of it like this: you upload your knowledge ‚Äî a PDF, a website, some training docs ‚Äî and Pickaxe turns it into something interactive that other people can actually talk to and get value from.

The thing that makes Pickaxe stand out from just... using ChatGPT directly... is what happens *after* you build it. You can sell your AI tools as a product, setting up bundles, prices, usage limits, and accounts. That's the interesting part. You're not just using AI for yourself. You're turning it into something that earns.

## How Does It Actually Work?

The workflow is pretty straightforward, which is kind of the point.

**Step 1: Build your Pickaxe.** You either start from a template or describe your idea and let their AI builder generate a prompt for you. Upload whatever knowledge base you want ‚Äî documents, website URLs, even YouTube videos. Customize how it responds, what it knows, and how it looks.

**Step 2: Put it in a Studio.** A Studio is a standalone AI web-app. It is a simple way to host and sell your Pickaxes to other people. Studios allow you to bundle tools, customize a page, manage users, and collect payments. So your Studio is basically your storefront ‚Äî your branded corner of the internet where people come to use your AI tools.

**Step 3: Deploy it everywhere.** You can deploy your chatbots on websites, custom domains, Slack, WhatsApp, or via email. Embed it on Wix, WordPress, Squarespace, Webflow ‚Äî basically anywhere that supports embeds.

**Step 4: Make money.** Set up subscription tiers, paywalls, usage limits. Stripe integration handles payments. Done.

It sounds almost too simple, but that's genuinely the flow. The platform is built so that the hard part ‚Äî the infrastructure, the AI plumbing, the payment processing ‚Äî is already handled for you.

## The Monetization Angle ‚Äî This Is Where It Gets Interesting

Let's be real: a lot of no-code AI tools let you *build* stuff. Fewer of them let you actually *sell* it in a meaningful way.

Pickaxe is built around monetization from day one. You can monetize your Pickaxes through subscription fees when you put them in a Studio, using Stripe to support payments. You can set prices and usage limits however you want, create multiple different paid tiers, and even put specific tools behind paywalls.

The revenue share model is worth paying attention to too. On the Free plan, Pickaxe takes 50% of what your Studio earns. Gold drops that to 10% (you keep 90%). Pro brings it down to 8%. So as you scale up what you're earning, upgrading the plan starts paying for itself pretty quickly.
